# 📊 Infini Telco Customer Segmentation & Product Categorization

## Overview

This project analyzes B2B telecommunications transaction data to identify customer segments and high-performing product categories using **Machine Learning (K-Means Clustering)** and **Pareto Analysis**.

The objective is to help Infini Telco improve customer retention, targeted marketing, cross-selling opportunities, and product portfolio management through data-driven insights.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Infini Telco faced several challenges:

* Limited visibility into customer purchasing behavior
* Inadequate product performance insights
* Difficulty identifying high-value customers
* Limited capability for targeted marketing campaigns
* Lack of data-driven product portfolio strategies

This project addresses these challenges through:

* Customer Segmentation
* Product Categorization
* Revenue Contribution Analysis
* Strategic Business Recommendations

---

## 🚀 Project Objectives

### Customer Segmentation

* Segment customers using K-Means Clustering
* Identify optimal clusters using Elbow Method and Silhouette Score
* Discover high-value customer groups

### Product Categorization

* Perform Pareto Analysis on product categories
* Identify core and long-tail products
* Analyze revenue concentration across categories

### Business Insights

* Profile customer purchasing behavior
* Generate strategic recommendations for marketing, sales, and retention

## 🔧 Feature Engineering

| Feature            | Description                                    |
| ------------------ | ---------------------------------------------- |
| Invoice Flag       | Identifies customers with more than 3 invoices |
| SKU Flag           | Measures product purchasing diversity          |
| Customer Spend     | Total spending per customer                    |

## 📈 Analytical Methodology

### 1. Customer Segmentation

**Technique:** K-Means Clustering

**Features Used:**

* Gross Turnover
* Customer Spend
* Product Categories
* Invoice Flag
* SKU Flag

---

### 2. Product Categorization

**Technique:** Pareto Analysis

**Purpose:**

* Identify top-performing product categories
* Analyze revenue concentration
* Detect core and long-tail products

---

### 3. Model Selection

#### Elbow Method

Used to determine the optimal cluster range by evaluating Within-Cluster Sum of Squares (WCSS).

**Selected Number of Clusters:**

* K = 10

---

### 4. Cluster Profiling

Customer clusters were profiled based on:

* Spending behavior
* Revenue contribution
* Product preferences
* Purchasing patterns

---

## 📊 Key Findings

### Revenue Concentration

* Total company revenue exceeded **RM931 million**
* Cluster 3 contributed more than **50% of total revenue**
* Top 20 VIP customers generated a significant share of revenue

### Product Performance

* Core products generated approximately **RM522.41 million**
* Around **8 product categories contributed nearly 80% of total turnover**
* Postpaid mobile plans emerged as the highest-performing category

### Customer Behavior

* Distinct purchasing patterns were identified across customer segments
* Lower-tier customers primarily purchased low-value or long-tail products
* Strong opportunities for cross-selling and upselling were identified
